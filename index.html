Building Global Standards for Web Digital Identity

The Web Identity & Credentials Adoption Community Group — An initiative of the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C).

Get Involved Today →

Our goal is to create and promote global web standards for identity and credentials (like Verifiable Credentials and Decentralized Identifiers).

Our Core Mission

WICA is dedicated to accelerating the maturation and adoption of open, interoperable, and privacy-preserving identity technologies.

Strategic Focus Areas

WICA helps developers and organizations adopt common digital identity standards. For example, we help determine how a web browser or app handles digital credentials universally and securely.

Technology Interoperability

We concentrate on ensuring that identity solutions work seamlessly across all browsers, devices, and platforms without friction.

Developer Experience

We prioritize creating best-practice guides, toolkits, and educational resources to empower developers to easily implement emerging standards.

Global Reach and Neutrality

Global Mandate

Our standards are designed for universal application and are not tied to the legal or governmental requirements of any single country or jurisdiction.

Independent & Neutral

Run by the W3C, we operate as an independent, international standards body, focusing solely on the health and security of the open web.

Driving Practical Adoption

WICA assists developers and organizations in adopting common digital identity standards in a cohesive, secure manner.

For example

"Defining how web browsers or applications securely and universally handle digital credentials across different platforms."
